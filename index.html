Our Sweet Legacy

Welcome to Mithaas Sweets, a family-run sweet shop with a rich heritage dating back to 1937. Currently headed by Mr. Sanjay Singhania, an MBA from FMS Delhi, we continue to serve authentic Indian sweets and snacks made with traditional recipes and the finest ingredients.

Our commitment to quality and taste has made us a beloved destination for generations of sweet lovers in Delhi and beyond.
