Our Team

Meet the dedicated individuals who work tirelessly to bring you the authentic taste of Mithaas Sweets

Sanjay Singhania

Owner & CEO

An MBA from FMS Delhi, Sanjay has been leading Mithaas Sweets since 2005, bringing modern business practices while preserving traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Suresh Kumar

Operations Manager

With over 15 years of experience, Suresh oversees the daily operations of the shop, ensuring everything runs smoothly from production to customer service.
